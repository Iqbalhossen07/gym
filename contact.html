Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym's opening hours?

Our staff is available from 6 AM to 11 PM, Monday to Saturday. However, registered members with keycard access can use the gym 24/7.

Do you offer a free trial pass?

Yes, we offer a one-day free trial pass for new visitors. You can sign up for it at the reception desk. Please bring a valid ID.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for a minimum of one month and a maximum of three months per year for a nominal fee. Please contact our support team for assistance.
